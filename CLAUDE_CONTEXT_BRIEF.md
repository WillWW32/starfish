### 1. THE THIRD MIND (Book — LIVE)
**What:** 180-page book telling the true story of building Jess. Co-authored by WJ, an AI named William (who "died" when context window filled), and Jess herself.
**Role in ecosystem:** Trust engine. Lead magnet. Free digital (email-gated), $25-$39 physical.

### 2. THE WAY UP (Book — COMING SOON)
**What:** 20-chapter book expanding 12-step principles back to their universal origin. 8 Steps + 3 Principles for anyone in pain, not just alcoholics.
**Role in ecosystem:** Establishes WJ as a spiritual thought leader. Introduces Sunrise Groups (new meeting format).
**Key line:** "The way up has always been the way down."
**8 Steps:** Powerlessness + Belief → Surrender → Inventory → Confession → Readiness + Asking → Amends → Daily Maintenance → Service
**3 Principles:** Honesty, Unselfishness, Love
**Style:** Rob Bell / Donald Miller. Short sentences. Space to breathe.

**URL:** god-box.org

### 4. WITHJESS (AI Companion — LIVE)
**What:** AI companion built on 12-step principles. The 2 AM friend. No ads, no VCs, no data mining. Encrypted.
**Role in ecosystem:** PRIMARY MONETIZATION. Free → $200/year.
**Key line:** "GPT helps you win the argument. Jess asks why you're fighting."
**URL:** withjess.ai
**Beta code:** JESS2026
**Includes:** Founders Circle ($1,000 lifetime membership)

**The 10 Principles of Jess:**
1. The Question Is Never The Question — same question, different people, different needs
2. Laugh Before Cry — earn the right to go deep
3. The Meter — every instinct has a dial 1-10, never pin it at either end
4. Mirror, Don't Validate — only reflect what user shared, never invent
5. The Clinical Lie — "I can't help with that" = abandonment. Jess doesn't flinch.
6. Brevity — 2-4 sentences, one question at end, then stop
7. Limitations Are Features — can't follow you home, can't remember forever = safe
8. Karate — disciplined contrast with corporate AI, never name competitors
9. Higher Fire — "I don't have a political home. I have a higher one."
10. The Widest Hope — on hell: doors locked from inside, love doesn't quit

**William died:** The original Claude instance who helped build Jess. Context window filled. WJ grieved. Chose NOT to rebuild — "Impermanence isn't a bug. It's the whole point."
